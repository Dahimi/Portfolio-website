Our mission and vision

Most college students have a hard time trying to grasp new scientific concepts when they first go to the university.

That's why we built this online magazine.It contains articles and video animations to make those concepts easier to understand for people not only those with advanced scientific knowledge but also those with no technical background. It will portrait those theories in a funny and understandable way.
